How to Buy Bitcoin (BTC) Securely

Please read the following instructions carefully to securely buy BTC with your debit card:

  1. Ensure you are on a trusted and secure website before entering your card details.
  2. Check the website's URL for HTTPS and a padlock symbol.
  3. Never share your debit card information with anyone.
  4. Be aware of the fees associated with the transaction.
  5. Use two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security.
  6. Once your transaction is successful, return to your dashboard and upload your payment receipt on the deposit page
